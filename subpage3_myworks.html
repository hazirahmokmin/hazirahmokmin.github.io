Digital Arts

Creating illustrations and artworks using digital technology, such as procreate, as a medium.

Stylised Scenes

Created digital stylised scenes using furnitures and everyday tools as an inspiration. This skill was picked up from one of the classes in Skillshare.

Illustrations

Using images that clients have sent, I've created digital illustrations, which can be used for other purposes such as card greetings, or even as a t-shirt print.

Card Greetings

Created digital card greetings which can be used as a festive greeting for family and friends.

Digital Textures

With different tools available on Procreate, I created digital textures which can be used to further enhance my designs.

3D Modelling

Modelling real-life objects into digital copies using Blender.

Toy Excavator

Due to the intricate details of the toy excavator, such as the bucket and the small items on the side of the track, it requires creative skills to create these objects.

Shiba Inu Plushie

It was important to ensure the scale of every objects on this plushie is as accurate as possible to ensure that it looks like the intended object. This was especially hard for its snout.

Van

While modelling each objects that makes up this van was relatively doable, it was more important to get the textures of them right, especially for the windows.

Gorgeous Doughnut

Are you craving for a doughnut now? But all jokes aside, it was for this object when I got the opportunity to use the sculpting tool in Blender to make that dripping part of the icing.

Watercolour Paintings

Using watercolour as a painting medium to create art pieces for card greetings and typography background.

Beauty of White

Displayed the beauty of whites against black with accents of gold.

Happy Birthday

Painted loose florals at the corner as a decorative element of the "Happy Birthday" greeting.

Thank You

Flowers were painted along the borders of the middle typography of a "Thank You" card.

Life is A Journey

Painted a floral border around the typography, making it the perfect gift for a friend.

Embroidery

Using needle and threads to create artworks on a fabric tote bag as the canvas.

Autumn All Year

Embroidered ranges of yellow and orange flowers within a border to portray the beauty of autumn colours.

Cat On A Bike

Embroidered a bicycle with a cat sitting on its seat for a bicycle-lover friend.

Drum Roll

Embroidered a whole drum set for a drum-fan friend. Because of its intricate details, it took me almost a week to complete this piece.

Spring Around

Who doesn't love flowers? Embroidered florals as a border around the letter "a".
